Our supporters have given generously to our work around the world. We want to ensure they’re aware we exercise sound stewardship of their money and are delivering on our mission to serve those in need. In this regard, the role of Action for Humanity’s board of directors is incredibly important. Their active participation and oversight ensures we never lose sight of our vision.

Some of their role include

  • visibility
    Ensure the expected standards of governance, accountability and transparency are met.
  • fact_check
    Approve budgets, audited financial statements and governance policies.
  • gavel
    Ensure all by-laws and policies — including those addressing conflict of interest, code of ethics — meet today’s standards.
  • verified
    Provide general oversight and create a culture of excellence within the organization.
